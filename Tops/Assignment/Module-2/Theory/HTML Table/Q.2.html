What is the difference between colspan and rowspan in tables? Provide examples. Ans. 1. colspan :- It allows a single cell to span across multiple columns. Used when you want a cell to take up more than one column. Example of colspan:
Header 1 & 2 Header 3
Row 1, Col 1 Row 1, Col 2 Row 1, Col 3
2. rowspan :- It allows a single cell to span across multiple rows. Used when you want a cell to take up more than one row. Example of rowspan:-
Header 1 Header 2
Row 1 & 2, Col 1 Row 1, Col 2
Row 2, Col 2
